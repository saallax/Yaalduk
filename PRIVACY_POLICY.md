
# Privacy Policy for Yaaldug

**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023  
**Last Updated:** May 20, 2024

Welcome to **Yaaldug**, a digital learning platform dedicated to providing high-quality educational content to the Somali community. Your privacy is critically important to us.

This Privacy Policy explains how Yaaldug ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application and services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users.

### A. Personal Information
When you create an account, we may collect:
*   **Identity Data:** Your name, email address, and user role (student or teacher).
*   **Profile Data:** Your profile picture, avatar preferences, and biography.

### B. Usage and Educational Data
To track your learning journey, we collect:
*   **Enrollment Data:** Courses you have joined or purchased.
*   **Progress Tracking:** Which lessons you have completed and your percentage of progress in each course.
*   **Certificates:** Records of completed courses and issued certificates.

### C. Payment Information
For premium courses and subscriptions using services like **ZAAD, EVC Plus, or e-Dahab**:
*   We collect **Transaction Reference Numbers** and payment status.
*   **Note:** We do NOT store your mobile money PINs or sensitive financial access codes. Payments are processed via secure third-party gateways or manual verification of references.

### D. AI Tutor Interactions
When you interact with the **Yaaldug AI Tutor**:
*   We collect the text of your queries to provide educational responses.
*   These queries are processed using **Google Gemini API**.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:
*   **To provide services:** Managing your account, enrolling you in courses, and tracking your progress.
*   **To improve learning:** Using AI to answer your questions and personalize your experience.
*   **Communication:** Sending notifications about course updates, new features, or technical support.
*   **Safety & Security:** Verifying payments and preventing fraudulent activity.

---

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

**We do not sell your personal data to third parties.**

We only share data in the following circumstances:
*   **Service Providers:** We use third-party tools like Google Firebase (for hosting/database) and Google Gemini (for AI tutoring). These providers access data only to perform tasks on our behalf.
*   **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations or protect the rights and safety of our users.

---

## 4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:
*   **Encryption:** All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using Industry Standard SSL/TLS.
*   **Access Control:** Access to user data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who need it to perform their jobs.
